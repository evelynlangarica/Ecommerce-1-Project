Thrifty Nifty Books

About Us

Thrifty Nifty Books wanted to create a virtual space where book lovers all around can Donate, Sell and Buy used books. Used doesn't necessarily worn out physically. Rather, it means that a book has travelled from the printing press to a home. A reader has enjoyed that book and wishes to pass it on to a new home. We truly believe that books are keys to our own little worlds. We can exit reality and travel to any parallel universe. We appreciate everyone who supports our small women-owned business!
